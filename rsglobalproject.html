RS Global Website Revamp


Redesigning the RS Global website and revamping its content architecture to increase user engagement and conversion rate.

UX Designer

Freelance Project

4 months (2022-2023)

Figma, WordPress, Elementor

Project Overview


Context

RS Global, a Dallas-based artificial turf company, wanted to revamp their website to simplify user navigation and encourage conversions. They wanted to add new pages that focused on the residential side of the business on top of the school and stadium-oriented projects, and consolidate the numerous Contact forms into a multi-step form for different types of projects (installations, maintenance, removal).

Brief

  • Incorporate new pages into the existing information architecture and revamp the site's design to facilitate easier navigation.
  • Combine the various Contact forms into one unified multi-step form to reduce confusion and shorten the path to conversion.

Design Decisions

Q: How to improve user navigation?

A: Reduce the number of dropdown menus and simplify nav link labels to prioritize recognition and aid findability

Reducing dropdown menus also reduces the number of clicks for the user when they navigate the site. Nav link labels are reworked to be clear and concise to help users know exactly where they are and where to go.

Q: How to encourage user conversions?

A: Refine CTA content, unify all Contact forms into one to minimize user navigation and confusion

There are several different Contact forms for each of RS Global's services. Consolidating all this into one form reduces confusion and aid findability if the user accidentally exits the process.
